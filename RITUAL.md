# The Pangaea ritual

The war is against Resistance: the force that makes you redesign the site
instead of writing the essay. It is strongest right before the work that matters
most. You do not beat it by feeling ready. You beat it by showing up.

## The identity

I am someone who ships.

Every piece is a vote for that person (Clear). The frame is not "I want to write
1,000 essays." That is a goal, and goals are for people who have not decided yet.
The system is: ship daily. The 1,000 is the exhaust, not the aim.

## Three teachers, one doctrine

- **Pressfield (The War of Art): turn pro.** Show up every day, sick or tired or
  uninspired. The amateur waits for the muse; the professional keeps office hours,
  and the muse learns where to find them. Resistance is a compass: the more you
  feel it, the more important the work it guards.
- **Kleon (Show Your Work / Keep Going): ship something small every day.** Show
  the process, not only the trophy. A daily dispatch beats a rare masterpiece.
  Forget the noun, do the verb. Become findable.
- **Clear (Atomic Habits): systems over goals.** Scale the habit down to two
  minutes so starting is never the problem. Make it obvious, easy, and visible.
  Never miss twice.

## The schedule

- **DAILY, the floor, non-negotiable:** ship ONE small thing. A seedling essay,
  a note, a song, a screenshot. The two-minute version counts. The rule is never
  zero. Touch the work and put something into the world every single day.
- **WEEKLY, the drop, the sokpop cadence:** every Friday, one real Pangaea piece
  across any pillar, an essay or a song or an app. Small scope, fixed cadence,
  relentless. 52 drops a year. The body of work is the asset.
- **NEVER MISS TWICE:** a missed day is human. A second missed day is the start
  of the old life. Miss once, ship twice tomorrow.

## The Resistance protocol

When you feel the pull to build the system instead of feed it, to research one
more blog, to perfect the graph: that pull IS Resistance, and it is pointing
straight at the thing you must do. Name it out loud ("this is Resistance"), sit
down, and ship the small thing anyway. Action is the only antidote. Feeling
follows.

## The container

Same time. Same place. Before the noise (Kleon's bliss station). You are not
deciding whether to ship; you already decided. You are only executing the ritual.

## The proof

`/work` lights a cell for every published piece. The grid is the habit tracker:
the satisfying, visible evidence that the person who ships is real. Watch the
field fill. That is the scoreboard, not the follower count.

## Pangaea is the collective

sokpop is four artists shipping a small game on a relentless cadence, and the
cadence is the brand. Pangaea is that for internet creativity, a studio of one
(for now) shipping essays, songs, and apps on a fixed drop. The relentlessness
is the identity. Patrons can fund it later; the work comes first.

## Turning pro, today

The professional starts before they feel ready. The smallest first rep is one
seed promoted to a post. Do it now. Not because it is good. Because you are
someone who ships.
