Welcome to Pangaea.

This is the first rift — the small opening before things drift apart. Each week
I'll send three things worth your attention from the three places I keep looking:
software, writing, and music. Sometimes a picture, a video, a quote.

If you only read one thing here, read the essay. If you only listen to one
thing, listen to the song. If you only download one thing, get the app. The
order doesn't matter; the bundle does.

Onward.
